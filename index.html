Why?

Rapid socio-economic changes combined with climate change, diseases, poverty, and high energy costs threaten food and environmental security. Solutions are available but they require multi‐disciplinary and multi‐stakeholder action within the natural unit of water management--the catchment.

About Us

IGFConsultancies.net works with laboratories in developing countries to accelerated capacity building using information and communication technologies (ICT).

Goal

Improve food production and management of vector-borne diseases in catchments while protecting people's quality of life such that no one loses and, if possible, everyone gains over time.

Approach

Capacity building and transferring technologies that improve resilience to emerging threats.

BirdCal

BirdCal version 1.3 now provides information on over 80 birds sighted on the Queensland Sunshine Coast with 12 birds featured as monthly calendar overlays that includes a new option for annotating sightings with your own photos, category/classifier and location.

Become familiar with local species and record your discoveries with BirdCal.

Getting Started

BirdCal opens at the current month with the current day highlighted in red. Swipe left or right to view the preceding or following months and their default photos. Tap the bird to display options:

  • browse photos or tap one to change the display for the current month;
  • view Wikipedia information;
  • create/view records of your own sightings and annotate observations; or
  • learn more about BirdCal.

Favourite viewing locations include Australia Zoo, Ben Bennett Park, Fraser Island, Kondalilla Falls, Lake Baroon, Lake Currimundi, Maleny Botanical Gardens, Mapleton National Park, Maroochydore Botanical Gardens, Mary Cairncross Scenic Reserve, Noosa Everglades and National Park, Point Cartright, and Pumicestone Passage.

Dedication

BirdCal was developed as a free–public service to commemorate Cyril Ferris and the founding members of the N.S.W. Gould League who brought environmental education to schools.

Privacy Policy (summary)

BirdCal treats User Personal Information with respect. It is not sold, rented, exchanged, communicated to 3rd parties or "finger–printed" for tracking purposes. So privacy should not be a concern especially since BirdCal aims to education and help you enjoy birding. That said, no data is 100% safe on the internet. You can choose not to annotated sightings. On the other hand, if you use this option, BirdCal asks for your permission (a one–off request) to access your camera, photo library and location, i.e., you decide the services BirdCal accesses. If you wish to chance a permission, delete BirdCal, re–install the app then tap "Don't Allow" or "OK" when one of the permissions is requested. Note: BirdCal does not require sensitive information. However, you may edited or deleted records subsequently. Under "Your Sightings" tap a record to display a detail view, tap "Edit" then "Delete". Latest revisions to the Privacy Policy are posted under the GitHub BirdCal project and highlighted in red.
